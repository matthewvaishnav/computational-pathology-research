# HistoCore Logo Tagline

## Recommended Tagline
**"Computational Pathology Framework"**

## Alternative Options

1. **"AI-Powered Pathology Analysis"** - Clinical focus
2. **"Pathology AI Platform"** - Shortest option
3. **"Deep Learning for Histopathology"** - Research focus
4. **"Production-Grade Pathology AI"** - Most descriptive

## Rationale for Recommendation

"Computational Pathology Framework" is recommended because:
- ✅ **Professional** - Appropriate for academic and clinical settings
- ✅ **Accurate** - Precisely describes what the project does
- ✅ **Concise** - Fits well on a logo without being too long
- ✅ **Technical** - Appeals to the target audience (researchers, developers)
- ✅ **Established terminology** - "Computational pathology" is a recognized field

## Logo Design Considerations

### Color Palette Suggestions
- **Primary:** Deep blue (#1E3A8A) - Trust, professionalism, medical
- **Secondary:** Teal (#14B8A6) - Technology, innovation
- **Accent:** Orange (#F97316) - Energy, attention (for highlights)

### Visual Elements
- Microscope or slide icon
- Neural network nodes
- Tissue/cell patterns
- Abstract geometric shapes representing AI/ML

### Typography
- **Main title:** Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto, Montserrat)
- **Tagline:** Lighter weight, same font family
- **Style:** Clean, professional, technical

## File Formats Needed

For professional use, create logos in:
- **Vector:** SVG, AI, EPS (scalable)
- **Raster:** PNG (transparent background), JPG
- **Sizes:** 512x512, 256x256, 128x128, 64x64, 32x32 (for favicons)

## Brand Guidelines

### Do's
✅ Use on white or light backgrounds  
✅ Maintain minimum clear space around logo  
✅ Use approved color variations  
✅ Scale proportionally  

### Don'ts
❌ Distort or stretch the logo  
❌ Change colors arbitrarily  
❌ Add effects (shadows, gradients) unless approved  
❌ Place on busy backgrounds
